Minecraft Speedrun Guide

206634IAIB

In this guide you will learn how to speedrun the game Minecraft. The goal is simple: kill the Ender Dragon!
First, however, you will need to buy a copy of the game here

For the speedrun you will need the following items:

Item Quantity Do you really need it
Water Bucket 1 No
Eye of Ender 0-12 Yes
Bed 10 Yes

Find your way to the stronghold and in it find the room with the end portal.
Fill in the portal frame with the Eyes of Ender you hopefully have.
This opens the portal to the End dimension and you are ready to fight the Ender Dragon.

The best way to defeat the Ender Dragon is by using some big explosives, AKA: beds!
For whatever reason beds tend to explode when someone tries to sleep in them in the End dimension.
This makes for an excellent tool for dealing high amounts of damage to nearby creatures.

And you've done it! You've defeated the Ender Dragon in what is hopefully a world record time!
And now the only thing left to do is to do it all over again and get a better time!
